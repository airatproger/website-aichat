FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is AI Chat?

AI Chat is a Chrome extension that brings powerful AI tools directly into your browser sidebar, allowing you to chat, summarize content, generate images, and work more efficiently without leaving your current page.

How does the free plan work?

New users receive free tokens to explore the product and test its core features before choosing a subscription plan.

What AI features are included?

AI Chat includes conversational AI, web page analysis, file attachments, image generation, web search, voice input, and other productivity tools.

Can I upload files and documents?

Yes. You can upload PDFs, images, documents, and other supported files for analysis, summarization, and AI-assisted conversations.

Which browsers are supported?

AI Chat is currently available for Google Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers.

Is my data secure?

We take privacy seriously. Your requests are processed securely to provide AI responses, and we do not sell user data to third parties.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.

Do I need an OpenAI or Gemini account?

No. AI Chat works out of the box without requiring you to create separate accounts with AI providers.
